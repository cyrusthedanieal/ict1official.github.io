Our Lady of Penafrancia



Every 3rd Sunday in September, the Bicolanos celebrate a festival in Penafrancia's honor. Bicolanos who come for the yearly pilgrimage light candles and kiss the Virgin's picture during the course of the next seven days. This issue also has religious and cultural significance to the Bicolanos. The biggest cockfights of the year take place at the plaza every night, along with bicycle races, shows, and a lively boat race that takes place on the river before the fluvial procession.

The Image is carried by worshippers to the crowded shoreline at noon on the 3rd Saturday of each month. The procession starts when the picture is loaded onto the boat. With much splashing, return to the ancient chapel until the festival the following year.
